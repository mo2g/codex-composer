## Codex App Template

- Skills: `planner`, `implementer`, `resume-work`, `change-check`, `debug-investigation`.
- Source of truth: `docs/codex-task-card-workflow.md` and `docs/codex-debug-workflow.md`.
- Start non-trivial work with `planner`, then keep the change reviewable and bounded.
- Keep durable workflow state in `docs/_codex/<task-slug>/`, including `debug.md` for active debug work.
- Use `resume-work` to recover from artifacts and `change-check` for the final evidence pass.
- Keep merge manual.
